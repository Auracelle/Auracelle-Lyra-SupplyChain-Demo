    What is Continuity of Knowledge?
    IAEA inspectors maintain CoK through continuous monitoring instruments: cameras, seals, NDA measurements. When CoK is broken — cameras offline, seals tampered, access denied — the inspector loses verified knowledge and must re-establish baseline.

    This gap is exploitable. A CoK score below 0.65 indicates structural vulnerability to clandestine activity during gap periods. Lyra models CoK as a time-averaged integrity score across the inspection cycle.
